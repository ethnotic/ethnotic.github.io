About Us

Ethnotic is a duo that takes its inspiration from the essence of humanity and presents it to the whole world with ethno-atmospheric vibes by blending the endless melody offered by the universe with eastern melodies. With a mission to introduce the West to the rhythms and notes that assimilated the social life styles of different origins whom left their individual identities aside, Ethnotic presents these tunes, which are often heard in the east, to their listeners on an electronic basis.
